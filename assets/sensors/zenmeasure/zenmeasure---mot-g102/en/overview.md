## Technical Overview for ZENMEASURE - Mot G102

### Introduction
The ZENMEASURE - Mot G102 is a state-of-the-art sensor designed for high-precision environmental monitoring. This device leverages the Internet of Things (IoT) to offer superior capabilities in data collection and transmission, providing valuable insights into various physical parameters. ### Working Principles
The ZENMEASURE - Mot G102 operates based on a series of integrated environmental sensors. These sensors are capable of detecting and measuring parameters such as temperature, humidity, light intensity, and soil moisture. The sensor data is processed in real-time using the onboard microcontroller and transmitted wirelessly.

The device utilizes LoRaWAN (Long Range Wide Area Network) technology for data transmission, which allows it to send collected data over long distances at a low power consumption rate. The choice of LoRaWAN ensures that the sensor can operate efficiently in remote or rural settings where cellular connectivity may be less reliable.
